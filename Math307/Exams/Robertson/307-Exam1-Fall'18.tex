How do we find the dimension of a vector space?

\vfill


\part[5] True of false: $\det(A+B)= \det(A) + \det(B)$
\vfill



\part[5] True of false: The rank of an $m \times n$ matrix is the minimum of $m$ and $n$.

\vfill

\vfill
